We are from a small team of WeChat Mini Games. We hope that everyone can create their own mini games and share their own ideas, so we have implemented a game development tool with a very low threshold. When designing, we try to pay attention to the simplicity of the concept and at the same time It can also realize various types of games. It is still in the internal testing stage, and everyone is welcome to use it. Since the release of WeChat mini-games, WeChat mini-games have become a unique type of game. Ordinary game platforms are aimed at experienced players, while WeChat mini-games have a wider user base, including children and grandparents, so casual and ultra-casual games account for a large proportion of WeChat mini-games. On the WeChat mini-game platform, various types of games can be accommodated, so we want to launch a mini-game development tool, hoping that more people can release their creativity and spread their ideas on WeChat. The current tool is in internal testing. From the perspective of our user groups, students, designers, and game planners prefer it.
