Our organization has reached a cooperative relationship with GunFu Fighter, this page will be used to introduce the various features of the game GunFu Fighter

 

【story background】 The global atmosphere is shrouded in a highly toxic mercury vapor, and humans have to wear masks to live. With superpowers of time control, you are sent to investigate whether the church has anything to do with air pollution.

 

【Game Features】

1. Full bullet time, recreating movie-like fighting scenes in a minimalist style

2. A variety of weapons can be used

3. Super slow motion mode, immersive into the movie special effects scene

4. Find the lost historical documents of the church and uncover the church's conspiracy step by step
